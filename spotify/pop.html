Pop Music


Okay — I haven't listened to pop music in a while. Which is weird because I used to exclusively listen to pop. But since getting Spotify, my taste has definitely expanded (probably for the better). That isn't to say I don't still have some women who have a permanent spot on my list. Doja Cat changed everything with Planet Her. I fucking love that album. SZA and Kali Uchis have easily become favorites as well. SOS? Like Jesus Christ how did she do that? (I think I'm seeing her this September :0) Ariana Grande will always have a special place in my heart. When she put out Sweetener and thank u, next it was over for my earbuds. These days I usually only listen to her in moments of deep sadness...can't really explain that one. But just know if you hear me poorly singing along to "goodnight n go," it means that I am not doing well.
