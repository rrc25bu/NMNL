Rock Music


I think rock music is really versatile. You can listen to it when you're happy or angry, and it expresses how you're feeling perfectly in both scenarios. I grew up listening to classic rock on the radio with my parents, so my playlist includes the likes of The Beatles, Pink Floyd, and Fleetwood Mac. Some more modern rock that I've been listening to is from The Strokes and Radiohead — I know, more manipulator music. Red Hot Chili Peppers reserves a few spots on the list.
