Rap Music


I don't know how to explain rap. I also definitely don't think I should try. It speaks for itself in a way, and admittedly I look/sound dumb when I scream "Dreams and Nightmares" at in the car. Some of the most influential rappers are Kendrick Lamar and, for me, Meek Mill. Kendrick's lyricism is unmatched. The storytelling in his music is beautiful and I think anyone who has listened to his discography has been enriched. Meek is just from Philly pride. Championships is an amazing backstory on his life in North Philly and how he got to where he is today. One rapper who branches into pop and R&B genres is Tyler, the Creator. His music is so good in every aspect, especially when he collabs with others. Igor had its moment for a reason, and CALL ME IF YOU GET LOST: The Estate Sale is deservedly having one as well.
